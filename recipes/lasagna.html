Lasagna Recipe

Description

Hearty lasagna with a ground beef meat sauce and three cheeses layered with lasgna noodles.

Ingredients

  • PAM® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 1 and 1/2 cups part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh italian parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 and 1/2 pounds ground chuck beef (80% lean)
  • 3/4 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon dried italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans Hunt's® Petite Diced Tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 and 1/2 cups Hunt's tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 9 dry lasagna noodles, uncooked
  • 2 and 1/2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
